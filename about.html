Hi, I'm Madeline, a GIS Analyst and Environmental Scientist. I grew up in Michigan, and after receiving my degree in Environmental Science from the University of Michigan, I packed my bags and headed west to beautiful Seattle. I am most passionate about utilizing geospatial technology to help solve issues relating to water resources, public health, and environmental policy. Outside of work, you can find me hiking, training for marathons, or curled up with a good book on a rainy day.
