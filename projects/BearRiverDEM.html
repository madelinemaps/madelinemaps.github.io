Kungsleden Trail Map

(click on map to see full-size version) The Kungsleden is a 460km (286mi) trail that goes through Lappland in northern Sweden. In September 2023 I was lucky enough to spend a week hiking the first ~75 miles of the trail! And since then I've never been able to stop thinking about it!. While I tried my best to map all the known mountain station huts from the sources I had, undoubtedly several huts were left out from the map due to their exact coordinate locations being unknown. It is my hope to return and complete the full length of the trail one day!

This map was made using a Digitial Elevation Model and several different types of raster functions and blending modes, inspired by John Nelson, in order to create the look.
