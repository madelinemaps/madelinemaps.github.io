Cartography Overview

Cartography is the art and science of creating maps. This field involves the process of designing, analyzing, and producing visual representations of geographical areas. Modern cartography uses both traditional techniques and digital tools to create maps that help users navigate and understand the world around them. Whether it's topographic maps, thematic maps, or interactive web maps, cartography plays a crucial role in spatial information visualization.
